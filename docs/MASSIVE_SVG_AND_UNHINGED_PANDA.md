### Why "Unhinged Panda"?

**Old Name**: "Vulgar Panda"
- Too tame for the level of chaos achieved
- "Vulgar" sounds negative/offensive
- Doesn't capture the humor aspect

**New Name**: "Unhinged Panda"
- Conveys extreme behavior
- Implies chaotic energy
- Sounds funnier and more playful
- Captures the unpredictable humor
- Warns users what they're getting into

**Backwards Compatible:**

    DUMBED_DOWN = "dumbed-down"
